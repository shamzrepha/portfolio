ABOUT

I'm a thinker and problem solver first — an engineer who'd rather take a problem apart and build the solution than read about it. Robotics is my main field; it's not my whole identity.

I study Mechatronics Engineering at the Federal University of Technology, Owerri, where I lead the GDG and RAIN tech communities. Recently I helped lead my team to 1st & 3rd place at the CODET Engineering Competition with a PET filament recycling machine, qualifying for the national finals in Abuja.

My range runs from embedded firmware and circuits to hardware and full mechanical robots, with AI where it adds value. I take a problem, prototype it with microcontrollers and code, and iterate until it works. I also teach STEM across schools to pass it forward. I'm now looking for an engineering internship where I can bring that energy — thinking, building, leading — to a real team.
