ABOUT

I'm a mechatronics engineering student who'd rather build the thing — and bring people with me — than just read about it.

I study Mechatronics Engineering at the Federal University of Technology, Owerri, where I lead the GDG and RAIN tech communities. Recently I helped lead my team to 1st & 3rd place at the CODET Engineering Competition with a PET filament recycling machine, qualifying for the national finals in Abuja.

That build-first habit is how I work: take a problem, prototype it with microcontrollers and code, and iterate until it moves the way it should. I also teach STEM across schools to pass it forward. I'm now looking for an engineering internship where I can bring that energy — building and leading — to a real team.
